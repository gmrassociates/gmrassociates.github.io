Background

Employee benefits, human resource functions and resulting employer "tasks" are costly, time consuming and complex. Most employers purchase benefits without comprehensive information about the insurer, the coverages and/or the administrative requirements. After the "sale," employers generally find themselves alone in a sea of "alphabet soup:" COBRA, FMLA, ADA, QDRO, ERISA, HMO, PPO, MSA, FSA, HIPAA and HR all represent regulations and functions that use more employer resources with every day that passes. Some of these actually expose the employer to risk. Some require software and systems too costly for many employers to develop. Large companies have learned to outsource many or all of these functions to increase profitability. GMR provides clients with a single source of benefit and administrative services that allows business owners to increase profitability and focus on their core business interests.
